<h3>Diwali_Sales_Analysis</h3>

This project presents an in-depth analysis of Diwali sales data using Python. Leveraging powerful libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, the dataset (sourced from Kaggle) is cleaned, explored, and visualized to uncover meaningful business insights. 
Tools & Technologies Used
Python
Pandas – Data cleaning & manipulation
NumPy – Numerical operations
Matplotlib – Data visualization
Seaborn – Advanced visualizations

📊 Project Objectives
Clean and preprocess raw sales data
Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA)
Identify key customer segments
Analyze sales trends across different categories
Create visually appealing and easy-to-understand charts
Key Findings


Gender & Marital Status:
Married women are the dominant buyers with the highest purchasing power, making them the primary target demographic.


Age Group 26-35:
This age group shows the strongest purchasing behavior and generates the highest revenue across all product categories.


Top States:
Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka lead in both order volume and sales amount, representing the key geographic markets.


Product Categories:
Food, Clothing & Apparel, and Electronics & Gadgets are the top-performing categories with the highest sales and purchase counts.


Professional Segment:
Buyers from IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors demonstrate the highest spending capacity and represent high-value customers.


📌 Final Conclusion
The analysis reveals that married women aged 26–35 form the most valuable customer segment during Diwali sales. Additionally, Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka emerge as key markets driving both revenue and order volume.

High-performing categories such as Food, Clothing, and Electronics highlight strong consumer demand during festive seasons. Moreover, professionals from IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors show higher purchasing power, making them an important target audience.

Overall, businesses can maximize their sales by:
Focusing marketing strategies on young married women

Targeting high-performing states
Promoting top-selling product categories
Personalizing campaigns for high-income professional segments
